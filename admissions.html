Application Checklist

All international applicants must prepare the following documents (Ref: DIT/REG/INT001). Submit to admissions@dit.edu.my

✓/✗ Required Documents
Note: Sub-Saharan Continent applicants require a No Objection Certificate or Eligibility Letter. Affidavit for Name required if name differs between documents.

DIT/REG/INT001

International Registration Form 2026

Contains Student Registration Form, Enrolment Declaration, Acceptance Form, Undertaking Form, Refund Policy, and International Student Contract.

For International Applicants

Health Declaration Form

All international applicants must declare their health status. Required by EMGS Panel Clinic / University Health Centre as per Malaysian immigration regulations.

Medical Condition to DeclareYESNO

Applicants must also declare willingness to submit to Post-Arrival Health Examination and bear costs for any medical treatment required during studies.
